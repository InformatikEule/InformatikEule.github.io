Pictures from Curiosity:

Here you can explore the fascinating images captured by the Mars rover. But how do these images end up here? It's quite simple: I utilize the NASA API. NASA, the United States' national space agency, generously provides the world with these valuable images and data for free. Dive in and uncover the beauty and mysteries of our neighboring planet, thanks to the generous provision of free NASA data. Welcome to the world of Mars rovers and the NASA API!

Attention:

Today and yesterday probably won't work as the data isn't available yet. Try a few days earlier! The earliest available date is August 6, 2012.

Attention for mobile users: More than 4000 images per day can be returned! Be careful with your data!
