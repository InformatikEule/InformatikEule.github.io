Pictures from another Planet...

On this page, it is possible to view the pictures taken by the Mars rovers. The three rovers operated by NASA are equipped with cameras that daily transmit their captured images to Earth. These pictures are freely provided by NASA and can be obtained from an internet interface (API).

Attention:

More than 4000 images per day can be returned! Be careful with your Mobile-data!

Lets go:

First, choose the rover from which you would like to see pictures:
Curiosity Perseverance Spirit (operative from 2004-01-04 to 2010-03-22. No pictures between those dates.)
Then, select a date: and press send to see the Pictures.
